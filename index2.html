About UPInspect

UPInspect was built to solve a simple problem: blind payments. We believe you should always be able to read and verify a UPI QR code before your banking app forces you to pay.

Our platform allows merchants to generate clean payment links and cards, and allows users to inspect them safely.

Privacy Pledge
